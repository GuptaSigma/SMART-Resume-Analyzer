Why SmartHire.AI?

The Problem

  • Recruiters spend hours manually screening resumes.
  • Many resumes are AI-generated, hiding genuine talent.
  • Traditional ATS systems rely only on basic keyword matching.

The Solution

  • Automated parsing of PDF and DOCX for skills, experience, and education.
  • AI-based relevance scoring to match candidates to job descriptions accurately.
  • Advanced detection of AI-generated or fake resumes.

Gmail Setup Guide

Why App Password is required:

Gmail requires App Passwords for third-party applications. Your regular password will not work for SMTP.

Step-by-step instructions:

  1. Go to your Google Security
  2. Select 2-Step Verification
  3. Select App passwords
  4. Choose Mail and Other (Custom name)
  5. Enter "Resume Analyzer" and click Generate
  6. Copy the 16-digit password and paste it above
Tip: You cannot view it again after closing, but you can generate a new one.
