Gmail App Password Setup Guide

Why App Password is required:

Gmail requires App Passwords for third-party applications to access your account securely. Your regular Gmail password will NOT work for SMTP authentication.

Step-by-step instructions:

  1. Go to your Google Account Security page
  2. Under "How you sign in to Google", select 2-Step Verification
    • If not enabled, you must enable it first
  3. Scroll down and select App passwords
  4. You might be asked to sign in again
  5. Under "Select app", choose Mail
  6. Under "Select device", choose Other (Custom name)
  7. Enter a name like "Resume Analyzer" and click Generate
  8. Google will display a 16-digit App Password (e.g., xxxx xxxx xxxx xxxx)
  9. Copy this password and paste it in the Email Password field above (you can paste with or without spaces)
Tip: You can't view the App Password again after closing the window, but you can always generate a new one if needed.
