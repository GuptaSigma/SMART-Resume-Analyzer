Why SmartHire.AI?

The Problem

  • Recruiters spend hours manually screening resumes.
  • Many resumes are AI-generated, hiding genuine talent.
  • Traditional ATS systems rely only on basic keyword matching.

The Solution

  • Automated parsing of PDF and DOCX for skills, experience, and education.
  • AI-based relevance scoring to match candidates to job descriptions accurately.
  • Advanced detection of AI-generated or fake resumes.

Key Features

AI Authenticity Check

Detect AI-generated vs human-written content using advanced perplexity and burstiness signals.

Automated Ranking

Instantly ranks candidates as Excellent, Good, or Average based on customized job requirements.

Smart Parsing and Matching

Compare extracted resume skills directly with job descriptions to estimate candidate fit more accurately.

Automated Emails

Automatically trigger interview invitations or rejection emails based on the final AI resume score.
